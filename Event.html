1-day Pipeline Corrosion Management Conference

Venue: Corus Hotel, Kuala Lumpur
Date: 27th September 2017
Time: 9.00 am – 5.00 pm
This one day conference will focus on addressing the corrosion based issues associated with pipeline corrosion with a view to address these questions and fostering future partnerships. The technical knowledge base alone will be insufficient to address the pipeline related corrosion challenges within the current environment. Innovation across multiple disciplines combined with the building of sustainable partnerships between operators, service companies, vendors, contractors and academia are essential.

There are three sessions to the conference:
1. 1st session – Pipeline Corrosion Monitoring and New Technologies
2. 2nd session – Corrosion Management
3. 3rd session – Pipeline Inspection and Integrity Management

1-day Conference on Prevention of Loss of Primary Containment

Venue: Nu Hotel, Bintulu, Sarawak
Date: 31st October 2017
Time: 9.00 am – 5.00 pm
The Institute of Materials, Malaysia (IMM) jointly with industries today continue to promote and create awareness of the benefits, rewards, respect, and importance of skills development for Craft workers and Graduate Engineers in Materials Technology, Corrosion, Coatings, Welding, Vi-bration, Insulation, Inspection, Polymers, Composites, Concrete, Wood, Rubber, Scaffolding, Fabrication, Materi-als Testing, Failure Analysis, and Materials Research to ensure the Industry continue to improve its facility integri-ty and maintenance activities. IMM Vibration Conference

Venue: Corus Hotel, Kuala Lumpur
Date: 16th November 2017
Time: 9.00 am – 5.00 pm
The conference is aimed at sharing knowledge in the practical application of new vibration related technologies to industry and the promotion of emerging techniques in vibration analysis and design.
There are two sessions to the conference. The first session “Design Studies, Standards and Training” concentrates on verification studies to mitigate against vibration problems at the design stage of projects. The second session, “Vibration Measurement, Monitoring and Reliability” concentrates on the application of vibration measurement and monitoring technologies to in-service equipment with the aims of improving reliability, accuracy, cost effectiveness as well as troubleshooting known issues.

About Us

MATERIALS TECHNOLOGY EDUCATION SDN BHD (MTE) started its journey into the educational world on July 2, 2004, to reach its ultimate destination - to be a 100 percent skills-based university in Malaysia. MTE is a product of a joint venture between Inter Merger Group (IMG) & IMM Resources Sdn Bhd. Institute of Materials Malaysia (IMM) wholly owns the latter.
