CERTIFIED VIBRATION PRACTITIONER LEVEL 1

CODE: VP1

The quality of vibration analysis and condition monitoring depends to very large extent on the quality of the people who carry it out. It does not matter how good the monitoring equipment is or even how committed management is, the program will fall unless it is run by capable and dedicated people. What can be done to ensure the availability of capable and dedicated people? Professional certificate with performance objectives will be the answer.

Objectives                                                    

• To highlight the impact of Vibration Management on health, safety and environment

• To identify common Vibration problems on machinery & it's impact on productive

• To highlight the   importance   of Vibration Technology from the efficiency and productivity perspective

Course Contents

•   Introduction

• Maintenance programmers

• Condition Monitoring

• Condition Based Maintenance (CBM)

• CBM Procedure

Course Duration

4 Days

Pre-Requisites

SPM (Sciences stream or vocational or equivalent) with at least credit in English and Mathematics

Who Should Attend

Engineers, Supervisors, Inspections and Technicians

Certificate

Certified Vibration Practitioner Level 1
