COATINGS INSPECTION CERTIFICATION SCHEME

 CODE: CIL 1 /CIL2

The Coatings Inspection Certification Scheme is to train and upgrade individuals in coating inspection and quality control.

This scheme will equip the graduate with the knowledge and skills to demand sufficient authority for his decisions to be recognised by both clients and controller.

Objectives

·         Specify protective paint/coatings system to a variety of substrates

·         Supervise the preparation of subsrates prior to painting

·         Supervise the application of paint coatings

·         Conduct   inspections  and   quality   control to  satisfy government, industry  and  union minimum standards

·         Diagnose and rectify faults in paint coatings and communicate in written form

Course Contents

Introduction to Corrosion Composition & Manufacture of Paints Surface Preparation

Application & Storage of Paints

Coatings Selection

Paint Faults & Coatings Defects Test & Measuring Instrument Safety & Health

The Role of a Coating Inspector

Painting Specification

Course Duration

Level 1: 4 Days (Training and Examination)

Level 2 : 1 Day (Examination

Pre-Requisites

Level 1 : No previous nor experience required

Who Should Attend ?

The course is suitable for those persons who wish to begin a career in this field but have little or no experience and for Painting Inspector, Painting Supervisor, Technicians, Specifiers and Engineers who desire to broader their knowledge in the use of protective paints and coatings.
