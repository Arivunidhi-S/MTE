CERTIFIED COATINGS QUALITY CONTROL TECHNICIAN (QAQC)

CODE: CQCT

IMM Coatings Quality Control Technician (Quality Control and Quality Assurance) course mainly targeted  for candidates on the technician level before they can proceed to the inspector level. Graduate of the programme will acquire a Certificate of Coatings Quality Control Technician issued by IMM.

This is an intensive training  programme  of basic functions  of quality control of surface preparation, coating types & failure and inspection knowledge and criteria.

Course Contents                                              

•  QC vs QA                                                                     

•  Monitoring  Environmental Conditional

•  Reference Standards and Codes

•  Pre- Cleaning Inspection

•  Inspection  (Tools,Method And Sequence)

•  Surface Preparation

•  Coating Materials and Handling Practices

•  Application Procedure

•  Materials Traceability

•  Repairs And Remedial Coating Work

•  Documentation and Compiling Accurate Reports

•  Recommendation

Course Duration

2 Days

Who Should Attend

Blasters   &   Painters   Technician   and   Blasting   & Painting Supervisor who wish to upgrade their knowledge and skills to another level of competency.

Certificate

 Certified IMM Coatings Quality Control Technician
