CERTIFIED CORROSION TECHNICIAN

CODE: CT1/CT2

IMM Certified Corrosion Technician is a training course consists of classroom lecturers, case study, practical session and assessment. This course is designed to train those work involves in corrosion related field in the basics of corrosion and its control.

 Objectives

Upon  completion  of  this  training,  participants   will gain   an   understanding    on   key-point    corrosion monitoring  and  process corrosion  monitoring; how to retrieve  and change-out  of coupons and probes; how to carry out measurement  and interpretation of corrosion  rates; how  to  use retrieval  tools, service valves, back-pressure  pumps and surge tubes; how to  adopt  the  cathodic  protection   monitoring   and inspection  techniques; how  to  utilize  the  corrosion Inhibitor monitoring  and protective coating surveys.

Course Contents

• Fundamental of Corrosion

• Forms of Corrosion

• Corrosion Control Techniques

• GRP and Insulation

• Coatings - Metallic & Non Metallic

• Coating Application & QC

• Cathodic Protection Anode QC & Surveys

• Corrosion Inhibition & Preservation

• Corrosion Monitoring Techniques

• Scanning Verification

• Probes & Coupons - What It Monitors

• Equipment Set for Installation & Retrieving

• Safety Issues for Corrosion Technician

• Key Points - What It Monitor

• Ultrasonic - Effectiveness and Usage

• CP Monitoring System

• CP Surveys & Probes

 Who Should Attend

This   course   is   intended   for   all   engineers   who interested in the knowledge of corrosion monitoring and inspection and who wish to pursue a career as a Certified Corrosion Technician.
