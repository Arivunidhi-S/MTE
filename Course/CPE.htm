CERTIFIED CATHODIC PROTECTION ENGINEER

CODE: CPE

This is a 4 days training course consisting of lectures, classroom practical, case studies and knowledge assessment. It aims to train participants in the understanding of CP principles, to be capable to go to field, accomplish periodical survey, and have sufficient troubleshooting skills to handle problems within a CP system.

Course Contents                                                  

• Corrosion Fundamentals & Principles                        

• Cathodic Protection Fundamentals

• Field Measurements

• Stray Current Identification

• Installing Cathodic Protection Components

• Troubleshooting Basic Design of Cathodic Protection System

• Properties and Application of Sacrificial Anodes & Impressed Current Anode & Equipment

• CP Instrumentation & Their Applications

• Soil   Restivity   Measurements,   Pipe   &   Cable Locating & Current Requirement Testing

• CP   Potential    Measurement    Including    Close Interval Potential Survey (CIPS)

• Datalogging, Mapping with GPS & Coating Defect Survey (DCVG/Pearson)

• Quality Assurance & Quality Control

• Field Testing

• Record Keeping

Course Duration

4 Days

Who Should Attend?

Cathodic Protection Technician, Corrosion Engineers, Managers and all involved in the maintenance, design and specifications
